About Me

Personal & Professional Bio

My name is Anish Palleti, a junior in ISM 1, and this year I am exploring the topic of Low Income Tax Assistance. I chose this issue because I have past experiences doing taxes for low income people, and I wanted to raise awareness and accessibility to tax resources for them to make the idea of taxes less intimidating.

Mission Statement

My mission is to pursue my passion for finance with curiosity and dedication, challenge myself through ISM by growing my skills and knowledge, and use what I learn to make a meaningful impact on others.
