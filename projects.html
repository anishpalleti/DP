Projects

Original Work

Proposal

A free tax education website specifically targeted towards helping low income people be more informed about the tax system and the resources available to them. Research showed me that many low income taxpayers do not take advantage of free resources like the Volunteer Income Tax Assistance (VITA) program because of a lack of awareness. They pay unnecessary filing fees, sometimes to fraudulent preparers, and overall they do not maximize the money they receive from the IRS.

Proposal (Google Doc)

Set-up & Completion

Developed and launched the tax education website through research, interviews with professionals (e.g., EY, Temple LITC, VITA Dallas), and iterative design. Implemented informational pages and direction to resources; added content on fraudulent tax preparer awareness based on mentor and interviewee insights. Hosted on GitHub Pages.

Assessment

Awareness and accessibility as key issues addressed; continued refinement with mentor feedback for the final product.
